Frequently Asked Questions

Shin-Chan Star

1.What is shin-chan stars?
Shin-Chan stars are the rewards points that customers earn with each purchase. It can be redeemed for free items,discounts or other rewards at Nohara’s house.
2.How do I earn Shin-Chan Stars?
You get Shin-Chan Stars for every RM10 spent through Nohara’s house or in-store purchases. Please flash your membership barcode to earn Shin-Chan Stars for in-store purchase.
